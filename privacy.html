Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: your name, email address, profile information, and payment details (processed securely through Stripe). When you participate in challenges, we collect photos you upload, along with associated timestamp and geolocation metadata.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: operate and provide the Challengrs service; process payments and payouts; verify challenge completion through photo proof; communicate with you about your account and challenges; and improve our services.

3. Photo Data

Photos uploaded as challenge proof include timestamp and geolocation data to ensure accountability. Photos are visible to other participants in the same challenge. We do not sell or share your photos with third parties outside the context of your challenges.

4. Payment Information

All payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store your full credit card numbers, bank account numbers, or other sensitive financial data on our servers. Please refer to Stripe's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle your payment information.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with: other challenge participants (limited to challenge-related data); payment processors (Stripe); service providers who help us operate the App; and law enforcement if required by law.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to: access your personal data; correct inaccurate data; request deletion of your data; object to or restrict processing; and data portability. Contact us to exercise these rights.

9. Children's Privacy

Challengrs is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the App or by email.

11. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at hello@challengrs.app.
