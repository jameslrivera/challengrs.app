3. How Challengrs Works

Challengrs is a social accountability platform where users participate in personal challenges. Here is how the core service works:

  • Users create or join challenges with personal goals (e.g., exercise, reading, meditation).
  • Participants stake money from their in-app wallet as a commitment to their goal.
  • During the challenge period, participants upload photo proof of completing their daily goal.
  • At the end of the challenge, participants who met their requirements are considered winners. Those who did not are considered losers.
  • Winners receive their original stake back plus a share of the stakes forfeited by losers, minus any applicable platform fees.

Challengrs is a skill-based accountability platform. Success is determined entirely by whether a participant completes their self-set goals and uploads the required proof. There is no element of chance involved.
